Join the Fun

Kamp Kreativity strives to provide children across the city of Oakland and the wider East Bay with the opportunity to discover the joy in learning through our 3-day program. In partnership with the Junior Center of Art and Science, Kamp Kreativity strives to share the magic of learning while also raising funds for the program with the purchase of each ticket going directly to them along side underprivileged Bay Area schools. Each day offers something new and exciting, so make sure to grab a ticket and join in on the adventure!
