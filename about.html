Who is Kamp Kreativity?

Our Mission

Kamp Kreativity is a Bay Area-based organization that strives to give back to the community of Oakland and the wider East Bay through camps that promote learning and creative growth in the arts and sciences through various activities and projects in its annual camps.

Our Partner

The Junior Center of Art and Science (JCAS) is an Oakland-based program dedicated to providing equitable access to their programs which cultivate creative and curious exploration to all youth and their families throughout Oakland and the Wider East Bay. Their values focus on the necessity of the arts and sciences and work to help provide children of all backgrounds and economic standing with equal opportunities to learn and grow through these subjects.
